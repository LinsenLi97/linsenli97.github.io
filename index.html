About Me

I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Massachusetts Insititute of Technology (MIT) Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) department starting since 2019. I have got my master of science degree in MIT EECS in 2021. I got my bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering in Tsinghua University with the highest honor: Tsinghua Presidential Award in 2018 (only 10 undergraduate got this award each year). I love physics and I was the gold medalist in the 2015 Asian Physics Olympiad (8 students are selected on behalf of China). I like to play with the experiment and got the best score in experiment portion of the 31st China Physics Olympiad in 2014.
